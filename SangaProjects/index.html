Prediction of Clinical Outcomes

  • Worked with the statistics department at the Texas A&M University

  • Performed web scraping, data cleaning, data miniming, data visualization, and statistical methods on large datasets from Continous Glucose Monitors

  • Researched gaps and opportunities in time series data from glucose monitors to determine relationships with diabetes and other clinical outcomes

  • Utilized supervised machine learning to estimate future clinical outcomes in patients based on personal health and activity
